Project Lead

Krystal Strong

Co-Creator


Krystal Strong is an assistant professor in the Graduate School of Education at University of Pennsylvania. She holds a Ph.D. in Anthropology from the University of California, Berkeley. Her research and teaching focus on activism, the cultural and political power of youth, and the role of schools as sites of political struggle in Africa and the African Diaspora. A native of Philadelphia, Krystal is actively involved in community organizing work in her hometown and brings this commitment to local issues and communities to her scholarship and pedagogy. Learn more about her work here.


Project Team

Tauheedah Shukriyyah Asad is a doctoral student in the Lew Klein College of Media and Communication at Temple University. Her research interests include the intersections of media, education and activism; anthropology of media; digital media and social media culture, public sphere pedagogy, the impact of media and emerging technologies in Black communities. Tauheedah is a proud graduate of Florida A&M University’s School of Journalism and Graphic Communication. She holds a master’s degree in Media Studies and Production from Temple University. Diggin’ Philly: Developing a Framework to Document and Preserve Black Culture Through Media and Emerging Technologies was her master’s thesis project.

Ansharaye Hines is a Philadelphia native and teacher at Mastery Charter, Shoemaker campus. Ansharaye is a producer, editor, and videographer for the web series.

Monique Perry is a longterm Philly resident, educator, and Education doctoral student at U Penn. Monique is a producer, editor, and videographer for the web series.

Ivy Williams is an undergraduate student at U Penn and producing a documentary project on Philly’s “Black Bottom” for her thesis. Supported by the Penn Undergraduate Research Mentoring program, Ivy is a research assistant for the project and an assistant producer and videographer for the web series.

Vince Anthony is a Philadelphia musician, songwriter, and recording artist working as the Music Director for the web series.
